Privacy Policy

sTels ("we", "us", or "our") operates the sTels logistic technology Chrome extension (the "Extension").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Extension and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information through the Extension. However, we may collect non-personally identifiable information, such as usage statistics and crash reports, to improve the performance and user experience of the Extension. This information is collected anonymously and is not linked to your identity.

Log Data

We may collect information that your browser sends whenever you use the Extension ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Extension that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics.

Cookies

We do not use cookies to collect information when you use the Extension.

Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.
